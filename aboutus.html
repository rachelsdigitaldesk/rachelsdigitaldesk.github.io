RDD Story

Built from Courage, Driven by Dreams

I am Rachel, the heart behind Rachel’s Digital Desk. In 2020, I took my first leap in building my own brand, Prima Bella PH, alongside balancing my full-time academic responsibilities. On my own, I learned to wear every hat—creating, managing, and figuring things out as I went, one step at a time.

Fast forward to 2023, a CEO believed in me enough to bring me into her marketing team, sparking my passion for helping brands grow.

On May 29, 2025, I took an even bolder step and launched Rachel’s Digital Desk. Within weeks, I secured two major clients and built a like-minded team fueled by creativity and a shared vision for growth.

Today, we are proud to work with a number of amazing brands. It all happened so quickly, and my heart is full of gratitude— for the trust, the growth, and the risks that blossomed into opportunities. By God’s grace, I believe we’ll continue to face our fears, seize new chances, and celebrate even more wins—together.

Malayo pa pero malayo na.

Proverbs 16:3: "Commit to the Lord whatever you do, and He will establish your plans."

Rachel Ann Villa

Founder, Rachel’s Digital Desk
